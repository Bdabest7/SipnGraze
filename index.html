Welcome to Sip 'n Graze Studio

Hosted by artist Ella Roesner at the exclusive Hillcrest Farms venue, Sip 'n Graze Studio offers a unique painting experience. Unleash your creativity surrounded by panoramic mountain views and adorable mini highland cows!

Our classes, often timed with the sunset, provide a relaxing and inspiring atmosphere for all skill levels. Join us for an unforgettable artistic adventure.
