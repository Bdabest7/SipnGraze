Welcome to Sip 'n Graze Studio

Hosted by artist Ella Roesner at the exclusive Hillcrest Farms venue, Sip 'n Graze Studio offers a unique painting experience. Unleash your creativity surrounded by panoramic mountain views and adorable mini highland cows!

Our classes, often timed with the sunset, provide a relaxing and inspiring atmosphere for all skill levels. Join us for an unforgettable artistic adventure.

Our Story & Vision

Meet Ella Roesner, Your Guide to Creativity

Ella is a passionate local artist with a love for nature and a knack for teaching. She believes everyone has an inner artist waiting to be discovered. Her friendly and encouraging approach makes painting accessible and enjoyable, whether you're picking up a brush for the first time or looking to refine your skills.

"I started Sip 'n Graze Studio to combine my two passions: art and the serene beauty of Hillcrest Farms. There's something magical about creating art while surrounded by nature, especially with our charming mini highland cows as your audience!"

The Sip 'n Graze Experience at Hillcrest Farms

Hillcrest Farms isn't just a venue; it's an escape. Nestled in Clinton, TN, it offers breathtaking panoramic views that evolve with the seasons. Our studio space is designed to maximize your connection with this stunning environment.

  • Inspiring Views: Paint against a backdrop of rolling hills and stunning sunsets.
  • Miniature Highland Cows: Our adorable and friendly mini cows often graze nearby, adding a unique and delightful touch to your experience. They're quite the models!
  • Relaxed Atmosphere: We foster a no-pressure, fun environment. Sip on your favorite beverage (BYOB options often available), chat with fellow painters, and let your creativity flow.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the class price?
Everything you need to create your masterpiece! This includes a canvas, paints, brushes, and 2.5 hours of guided instruction from our artist.
Do I need any painting experience?
Absolutely not! Our classes are designed for all skill levels, from complete beginners to more experienced painters. Our friendly instructor will guide you step-by-step through the entire process, ensuring you feel confident and have fun.
Can I bring my own drinks or snacks?
Yes, please bring your favorite beverages (the "Sip")! We provide complimentary charcuterie boards (the "Graze") for you to enjoy while you paint. Please check class descriptions for any specific notes on alcohol for all-ages events.
Will we really see the mini highland cows?
Yes! Our studio is located at the beautiful Hillcrest Farms, which is home to a herd of adorable and friendly mini highland cows. They often graze nearby, providing a unique and charming backdrop for your painting experience. While we can't guarantee they'll pose for your painting, they are almost always part of the scenery!
What should I wear?
We highly recommend wearing comfortable clothes that you don't mind getting a little paint on. We do not provide aprons, and acrylic paint can be tricky to remove from fabric once it dries, so it's best to dress for the mess!
What is your cancellation policy?
We understand that plans can change. For a full refund, please cancel at least 48 hours before your scheduled class time. Cancellations made within 48 hours of the class may receive a credit to be used for a future class. Please refer to your booking confirmation for specific details and contact us directly for any changes.
